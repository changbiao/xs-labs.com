Unix manual pages Published on: Jan 3, 2012 @ 23:08 I just released a little commercial application on the Mac AppStore. It's a GUI front-end for the UNIX manual pages. It automatically searches your computer for all available manuals, and displays them in a nice way, ordered by sections. The following features are available:
  • Live search for specific manual pages
  • Search and highlight in manual contents
  • Printing support
  • Filtering by sections
You may purchase it from the Mac AppStore, for $1.99.
